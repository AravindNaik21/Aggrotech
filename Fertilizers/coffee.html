Coffee Fertilizer Recommendations

Proper fertilization is essential for healthy growth and optimal yield in coffee plants. Below are general guidelines for fertilizing coffee:

Fertilization Schedule

  • Pre-Flowering (Approximately 30 Days Before Flowering): Apply a balanced fertilizer with micronutrients to support cell division and flower induction. Incorporate boron (B), zinc (Zn), manganese (Mn), and copper (Cu) to enhance flower vitality.
  • Flowering Period (Approximately 30 Days): Maintain a balanced nitrogen (N) to potassium (K) ratio, ideally 1:1, to ensure growth and flower vitality. Seaweed extracts may support pollen quality and berry uniformity.
  • Fruit Set and Maturation (Approximately 150 Days): Increase potassium (K) and calcium (Ca) application to ensure proper fruit development and quality. Foliar spraying with potassium nitrate (Multi-K) can improve yield and bean size. It's recommended to start foliar applications 12 weeks after flowering, using a 4% Multi-K solution, followed by two more applications at 30-day intervals.

Fertilizer Types

  • Balanced Fertilizers: Use formulations such as 19-4-21+0.8B+0.04Cu+0.1Mn+0.45Zn to provide essential nutrients during pre-flowering.
  • Potassium-Rich Fertilizers: Apply potassium nitrate (Multi-K) during fruit set and maturation to enhance bean quality.
  • Organic Options: Well-decomposed compost or manure can be used to improve soil fertility and structure.

Application Tips

  • Distribute fertilizer evenly around the base of the plant, avoiding direct contact with the stem.
  • Water thoroughly after each application to help nutrients reach the root zone.
  • Monitor plant health and adjust fertilization based on growth, leaf color, and soil test results.
  • Consider soil pH and texture, as they influence nutrient availability and coffee quality. Liming may be necessary to correct soil acidity based on soil test recommendations.

Note: Soil conditions and nutrient needs can vary. It's advisable to conduct a soil test for precise recommendations tailored to your specific conditions.
