Fertilizer Recommendations for Orange Trees

Proper fertilization is crucial for maintaining healthy and productive orange trees. Below are guidelines to assist you:

Nitrogen (N)

Apply nitrogen fertilizers in January or February, just before the trees begin to bloom. The exact timing depends on your local climate and the trees' readiness. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Phosphorus (P) and Potassium (K)

Soil and leaf analyses should be used to monitor the phosphorus and potassium status of an orchard and to plan applications accordingly. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Soil pH

Maintain soil pH within the optimal range for citrus trees. Irrigation water in some regions is often high in bicarbonates, which can increase soil pH over time. Implement practices to manage soil pH effectively.
